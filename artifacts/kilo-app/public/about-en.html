About Kilo

Kilo specializes in light commercial vehicle rental for companies and institutions across Saudi Arabia. We provide practical, reliable transport solutions that support daily supply chain operations for distribution and service sectors.

Our core service is monthly rental of refrigerated and dry light commercial vehicles. Packages include maintenance, insurance, a replacement vehicle during breakdowns, and fleet tracking to help companies manage operations efficiently.

We serve food distribution companies, pharmaceutical and medical supply businesses, catering and hospitality providers, and retail operators that need consistent daily transport with stable operating standards.

Our refrigerated vehicles comply with SFDA requirements and feature high-performance cooling and thermal insulation to protect product safety during transit. We also provide dry vehicles for non-temperature-sensitive goods.

We believe companies need an operational partner, not just a vehicle supplier. Our monthly contracts are transparent and competitively priced, with ongoing support designed to keep your operations running without disruption.
